About

I’m Xuanzhou Chen, a second-year machine learning Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Ashwin Pananjady at Georgia Tech. My research sits at the intersection of machine learning theory, causal inference, and generative models. I study how to recover latent structure from high‑dimensional data and design algorithms with provable guarantees.
